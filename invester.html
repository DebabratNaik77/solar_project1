INVESTOR GRIEVANCE CONTACT

An Investor Grievance Contact is a designated point of contact within a company or a regulatory authority that investors can reach out to in case they have complaints, grievances, or queries regarding their investments. This contact is responsible for addressing investor concerns promptly and effectively, ensuring that investors receive proper assistance and resolution for their issues. Their role may involve investigating complaints, providing relevant information, facilitating communication between the company and investors, and ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements related to investor protection and grievance redressal. Having a dedicated Investor Grievance Contact demonstrates a company's commitment to transparency, accountability, and investor satisfaction.

REGISTRAR & SHARE TRANSFER AGENT

A Registrar & Share Transfer Agent (RTA) is a financial institution, typically a bank or a specialized firm, appointed by a company to maintain records of its shareholders and manage share transfers. Their responsibilities include maintaining an updated record of shareholders, processing transfers of shares, issuing new share certificates, handling dividend payments, and managing other related tasks like handling investors' queries and managing corporate actions such as mergers, acquisitions, and stock splits. Essentially, they serve as intermediaries between the company and its shareholders, ensuring smooth and accurate management of shareholding information and transactions.
